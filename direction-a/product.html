Suggested use

Start low, go slow

1

Begin gently

Take half a gummy (≈2.5mg THC) 30–60 minutes before bed, on a light snack. Give it time — gummies take 1–2 hours to peak.

2

Find your dose

Comfortable? Move to a full gummy the next night. Adjust in small steps — there's no rush to find your sweet spot.

3

Make it routine

Use nightly for 2–4 weeks. Cannabinoid benefits build with consistency, so a steady ritual beats the occasional gummy.

Common questions

Will Nightcap make me fail a drug test?

Possibly. Nightcap contains hemp-derived THC, which can trigger a positive result on standard drug screens. If you're subject to testing, we'd recommend our THC-free Mellow Mood instead.

Is this legal?

Nightcap is made from hemp containing less than 0.3% Delta-9 THC on a dry-weight basis, federally compliant under the 2018 Farm Bill. State laws vary, so we ship only where these products are permitted. You must be 21+ to purchase.

How long until it works?

As an edible, Nightcap takes 1–2 hours to take effect and lasts up to ~8 hours. Taking it with a little food can help absorption. For sleep support, the routine matters most — give it 2–4 weeks of nightly use.

Can I take it with other medications?

Cannabinoids can interact with certain medications. Please consult your physician before use, especially if you take other medication or are pregnant or nursing.
