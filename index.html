Our read on Bloom

Physician-grade hemp, made approachable

Bloom is physician-formulated and lab-tested, and the store should feel as calm and welcoming as the products themselves. All three directions lead with that — credible and transparent up front, warm in tone. What changes between them is the emphasis.

A few things about how hemp shoppers actually buy shaped every decision below:

1

People shop by how they want to feel — sleep, calm, focus, or relief. So every direction leads with shop-by-need.

2

Trust is what converts. Hemp buyers are skeptical — visible lab results and physician framing do the heavy lifting.

3

New customers get stuck on dosing. Plain "start low, go slow" guidance reduces hesitation and returns.

4

It's a regulated category. A clean 21+ gate and proper disclaimers aren't optional — and done well, they build trust.

5

Most visits are on a phone. Fast, mobile-first, and uncluttered is the baseline, not a nice-to-have.

What we'd build into the site — and why

These foundations come from how hemp shoppers actually buy and from the rules of the category. They carry across all three directions.

Shop by need

Sleep / Calm / Focus / Relief entry points — matching how people actually shop.

Lab results, front and center

A Certificate of Analysis on every product. Visible proof is the #1 trust driver in hemp.

Dosing guidance

A plain "start low, go slow" helper — it cuts new-customer hesitation and returns.

What to expect

Honest, wellness-framed guidance on how each formula is meant to feel — calm, rest, focus, relief — so first-time buyers feel confident.

Branded 21+ age gate

Required for the category — and a credibility signal when it's on-brand, not bolted on.

FDA disclaimer

The correct legal language, handled cleanly and unobtrusively. Non-negotiable for hemp.

A warm, plain-spoken voice

Credible facts, in the tone of a knowledgeable friend.

Email capture + welcome offer

"Join the Bloom Family," 15% off — builds the list and repeat purchases.

Bundles

Multi-pack value — the main lever to lift order value (our recommended alternative to subscriptions).

Self-serve promo blocks

Edit announcements and run specials yourself, no developer needed.

Keep your search traffic

1:1 redirects from the current site at launch, so existing rankings and links carry over.
