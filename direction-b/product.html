Frequently asked

Is Nightcap legal?

Yes. Nightcap is made from hemp-derived cannabinoids containing ≤0.3% Δ9-THC on a dry-weight basis, compliant with the 2018 Farm Bill. We ship within the US only and cannot ship to states where these products are restricted — your eligibility is checked at checkout.

Will it show up on a drug test?

Possibly. Nightcap contains THC, and THC can trigger a positive result on standard drug screens. If you're subject to testing, we'd recommend our THC-free Mellow Mood instead.

How long until it works, and how long does it last?

As an edible, expect onset in roughly 1–2 hours and effects lasting up to ~8 hours. Take it 30–60 minutes before bed, and don't redose the same night.

Can I take it with other medications?

Talk to your physician first — cannabinoids can interact with some medications. Don't use if you're pregnant or nursing. This product is for adults 21 and older.

Every batch has its own Certificate of Analysis from an independent A2LA / ISO 17025-accredited lab. Scan the QR code on your jar, or look up your batch number on our Lab Results page.
